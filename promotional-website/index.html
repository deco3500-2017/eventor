What is Eventor?



An application focused on connecting social gatherings to potential attendees who may be interested in those events but can’t find them due to not having the right platform or simply because they are new to that community and have no connections. Our application’s goal is to ease the user’s process of finding events by filtering them based on user’s location and interest.

Why eventor has been designed?



It’s hard to find community events or social gatherings that are based on our interests or find like-minded people unless we already have connections in that community or manually find one from social media. What if we don’t have connections in that community and can’t find those events?

How does eventor designed?



An application solely focused on connecting local social gatherings to potential attendees who are interested in those events but can’t find them due to not having the proper platform or merely because they don’t know anyone in that community.

Features

Find Events

You can find events based on your interest and your current location! Meaning, you don't need to scroll through events you don't like.

Express Interest

You can express your interest in attending that event and the host will be notified!

Create Your Own

You can also create your own event. All you have to do is fill a form and your event will then be published for others to see and attend!
